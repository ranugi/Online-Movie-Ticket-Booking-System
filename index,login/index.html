PRIVACY POLICY

FlixTicket Theatres values its relationships with its guests. FlixTicket Theatres has created this policy to describe its policies regarding its collection of information from consumers, its use of such information, and its privacy practices with respect to same. The information we collect from you is used to process your orders and to provide you with a more personalized, quick, convenient shopping and rewards experience. In order to make informed decisions regarding your personal information, FlixTicket Theatres strongly urges you to review this policy completely and carefully.

How FlixTicket Collects and Uses Data

For each visitor to our website, our web server automatically recognizes only the consumer's domain name, not email address. When you visit the FlixTicket Theatres website, FlixTicket automatically collects data on your website usage and online behavior, e.g., previous websites you visited, your computer's web browser, operating system, and IP address, how you navigate and interact with the FlixTicket site. FlixTicket may also collect additional information from you, depending on how you engage with FlixTicket. For example, FlixTicket may collect information from you if you post messages to our bulletin board, communicate with us through email or online comment cards, or participate in surveys or site registrations. While FlixTicket generally limits the use of such information to its own internal use to address guest concerns and improve the guest experience, FlixTicket may post guest feedback and comments on its website or on social media.

Sharing of Information

FlixTicket uses information collected from you in a number of ways, including, without limitation, to improve the content and navigation of FlixTicket's website and mobile applications, to assist in the marketing and promotion of films, events, and FlixTicket products in which you may be interested, to communicate with you for guest services purposes, and to prevent and detect fraud or other misuse of our website, mobile application and promotions. Further, FlixTicket routinely shares this information with third parties, such as motion picture distributors, industry data sharing services, and promotional and other business partners.



Third parties

FlixTicket retains third parties to place and manage advertisements on its website and mobile applications. These third parties may automatically collect information from you, through cookies and other technology, in order to provide you with advertisements for goods and services in which you may be interested. FlixTicket also uses third parties to assist it in providing services, fulfilling orders, Data and information requests, to improve your experience with the Applications and on FlixTicket's websites and for other business purposes. FlixTicket may share your personally identifiable information with these third parties for these purposes.



Website use by children under 13 years of age

FlixTicket does not specifically target its websites and applications to children under 13 years old. If you are under the age of 13, you should only use our website and applications under the supervision of a parent or guardian.



Security

flixtickettheaters.com has implemented security measures to protect against the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control. We protect the secure areas of our Site with a firewall. Although flixtickettheaters.com has implemented adequate security measures, the site has contracted Verisign to provide an extra assurance of security. Although, the site is completely virus-free, the User is advised to employ virus scans for extra security, as flixticket.com is not liable for any virus picked up at the time of transmission.



Deletion of Information and Records

While flixticket.com may track and store information, flixticket.com shall not be obligated to do so and may delete any information and records, in whole or in part, at any time.



Choice/Opt-out
